Brief Bio

I am Ravindrakumar Purohit, a research scholar currently affiliated with the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar. With a background in BE from GTU and an M.Tech in CSE specialized in Data Science from Nirma University, I bring a strong academic foundation to my research pursuits.

As a Junior Research Fellow, I am actively engaged in cutting-edge research in the fields of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Mining, NLP, and Machine Vision. My primary focus lies in the application of Machine Vision and its diverse applications. Prior to joining IIT Gandhinagar, I served as an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Technology, Ganpat University. With a passion for research and an extensive knowledge base, I strive to contribute to the advancements in these areas and make a significant impact in the field.


Vision: To lead the way in developing innovative solutions that harness the potential of AI, improving the lives of individuals and driving advancements in computing for a better future.

Mission: To create transformative technologies that empower individuals and shape the future of AI-empowered humanity and computing.
